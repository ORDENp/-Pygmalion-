# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a space based on:
- respect,
- dignity,
- openness,
- non-coercion.

---

## Expected Behavior

- thoughtful communication
- listening before responding
- respect for different perspectives
- responsibility for words and actions

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- manipulation or coercion
- dehumanizing language
- ideological pressure
- attempts to exploit the project or participants

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation
to protect the integrity of the project and its values.

---

## Final Note

This is not a battlefield.  
It is a garden.

Help it grow.
